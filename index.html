AR Glasses for Etiquette Training

Tagline: A fun way to level the playing field

Problem

More than 90% of early learners had their education disrupted by COVID-19, resulting in many missing vital early childhood experiences interacting with adults and peers outside their families. Many students are now lagging socially, and younger generations are often described as “shy.”

5 Whys

  • Why is Gen Z / younger kids described as more anxious?
  • Higher stakes for performance in everyday life
  • More things are documented online
  • Social media
  • Less opportunities for in-person interaction past 5 years because of COVID

Solution

Let's gamify social interaction and help children with social anxiety or speech delays feel less anxious while interacting with others!

Features

  • Real-time body language reader (Computer Vision trained on people)
  • Point system: earn points for following etiquette (no losing points)
  • Add friends: practice with friends – friends double points
  • Co-pilot mode: practice with AR person

Verbatim: The app allows kids to feel less anxious when interacting with others by giving live feedback on non-verbal cues and nudges.
